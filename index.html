With this game, you can practice the mathematical order of operations. You will be shown some numbers from 1 to 10, and you will have to select the correct operators to get the equation to work out.

You can change how many numbers are included in the equation with the drop-down menu on the bottom.

Right now, you can choose between these operators:

  • +: addition
  • -: subtraction
  • x: multiplication
  • /: division
